Welcome To Madras Junior College!

Madras Junior College (MJC) is one of the leading junior colleges in Madras.It follows the Higher Secondary Certificate Examination of Tamil Nadu Board and is founded on the motto of 'We are not what we know but what we are willing to learn!' MJC offers Science, Commerce and Arts streams with a focus on value-based education. It's an institution of higher learning that puts an emphasis on human enrichment and development.
